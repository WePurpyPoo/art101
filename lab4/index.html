Challenge

Choose a simple computer game and create pseudocode for it. Create a javascript file that has the pseudocode commented in.

Problems

We had a bit of trouble trying to figure out a day to day activity that people would need instructions for, as most such tasks are common sense. However, when we though about the lives of Santa Cruz students in particular, we realized we had a great resource available to us, an abundance of hiking trails! Going Hiking Pseudocode

			
			- Hiking Task List
				- Preparation Steps
					- Decide on where you want to go! Research hiking trails in the area, and select one that you think you would like!
						- Keep in mind the difficulty of the trek, or scenery you would like to visit!
						- Check the weather forecast! You don’t want to be surprised by rain on your day out!
						- Make sure you pack sources of water and food, hiking takes a lot of energy.
						- Bring safety equipment such as first aid in case you get hurt! Nature can be beautiful, but it is also dangerous.
				- Activity Steps
					- Arrive on the scene. Take a deep breath, your adventure is about to begin!
					- Be aware of your surroundings, whether it be dangerous animals or treacherous conditions.
					- Make sure you are hydrated, dehydration is very dangerous especially in the wilderness.
					- If you are new to hiking, take it slow! The ground can be less stable than expected, and getting a twisted ankle in the wilderness can be inconvenient.
					- Know your limits. You may feel invigorated on your trip, but you don’t want to use up all your energy before you make the trek back. 
						When in doubt, don’t be afraid to turn around! The trail will still be there another day.
					- Have fun! Immersing yourself in nature can be very refreshing from your day-to-day life, make sure you make the most of it!
